# Research Figures

This directory contains visual assets generated by the research simulations, supporting the findings in the IPHY and MEBA frameworks.

- **fig1_mode_distribution.png**: Distribution of entity modes.
- **fig2_wisdom_duration.png**: Duration of wisdom states.
- **fig3_valence_comparison.png**: Comparison of valence metrics.
- **fig4_crisis_patterns.png**: Patterns identified during crisis simulations.
